There are 5 links available in the site. They are Introduction, Theory, Objective, Experiment, Quizzes, Procedure. There is front matter or intro in the introduction. In the theory there is content about the experiment. The main objective of this experiment is mentioned in the objective tab. In the experiment tab all the functionalities are to be done. The quiz is conducted in quiz tab. The step by step procedure is mentioned in the procedure tab.

  • First select the language which you know better among english and hindi.
  • Next select the buttons appropriate to form the sentence in order.
  • Then the order of the words will be shown.
  • If a wrong sentence is formed, Re-form the sentence button is available for re-setting.
  • By clicking the check the correctness of this sentence button you can check whether the formed sentence is correct or not.
  • You can get the correct sentence by clicking get correct sentence button
  • If the formed sentence is correct then Right answer! in green color will be shown.
  • If the formed sentence is incorrect then Wrong answer! in red color will be shown.
